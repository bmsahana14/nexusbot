# TechNexus Community Events

## About Our Events

TechNexus Community hosts regular in-person and virtual meetups, workshops, and events designed to bring together tech enthusiasts, developers, AI practitioners, and innovators. Our events provide opportunities to learn, network, and collaborate on cutting-edge technologies.

## Event Formats

### In-Person Meetups
Join us for face-to-face networking and learning experiences. Our in-person events are held in various locations including Bengaluru and Chennai, creating local tech communities.

### Virtual Events
Can't make it in person? We also host virtual meetups that allow members from anywhere to participate and engage with the community.

### Workshops
Hands-on technical workshops where you can build practical skills and work on real projects with guidance from experienced practitioners.

## Past Events

### Code & Cold Pizza
A casual coding event combining programming, collaboration, and good food. Perfect for developers who want to code together in a relaxed atmosphere.

### Agentic AI Connect
Focused on the latest developments in AI agents and autonomous systems. Features talks from experts and discussions on AI applications and innovations.

## Event Topics

Our events frequently cover:
- **Artificial Intelligence**: Latest AI trends, applications, and research
- **Data Science**: Data analysis, machine learning, and analytics
- **IoT**: Internet of Things projects and innovations
- **Software Development**: Best practices, new frameworks, and tools
- **Entrepreneurship**: Building tech startups and innovation
